COVID Screener (Aug 2020)

As a part of a virtual hackathon event by Jumpstart, my group and I created COVID Screening, a startup geared toward school districts, workplaces, and organizations who need a tool to manage the safeness of all individuals attending school, work, or a specific public area. Our startup provides student health tracking through immense software and hardware integrations including bluetooth triangulation and daily surveying to ensure the maximum safety for individuals in highly-concentrated public areas such as school or work. This project received 1st Place in the 'Best Business' category at the 2020 Jumpstart EdTech Hackathon.
